## What to Skip

- **Pod-based "frother" accessories marketed for specific machines.** They're overpriced, break within months, and a $10 handheld wand does the same job.
- **Cheap blade grinders bundled with coffee makers.** Blade grinders chop unevenly, produce inconsistent particle sizes, and quietly ruin every brew. If you're upgrading anything, upgrade the grinder — but don't buy a budget blade grinder thinking it'll help.
- **Decorative pour-over sets with no temperature control.** A beautiful ceramic dripper paired with a kettle that boils to 212°F with no control is just expensive frustration. Temperature matters more than aesthetics.
