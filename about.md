Kitchen Finds is a product recommendation site focused on kitchen gadgets, tools, and appliances that are actually worth buying: all under $50.

## Who runs this site

Kitchen Finds is run by the Kitchen Finds Team, a small operation built around one idea: most people don't need to spend $150 on a stand mixer or a chef's knife to cook well. We research and write every roundup ourselves, drawing on manufacturer specs, verified buyer reviews on the retailers we link to, and (where we've used a product ourselves) our own kitchens. See our [how we pick guide](/how-we-pick/) for the exact criteria we use.

## Why under $50

We picked the under-$50 price point on purpose. It's the range where most home cooks, apartment dwellers, and college students actually shop: low enough to buy without agonizing over it, high enough that the product has to be genuinely good to earn a spot on a list. Above $50, you're often paying for a brand name or features a home kitchen doesn't need. Below it, quality gets inconsistent fast, which is exactly why our selection criteria matter more at this price, not less.

This site contains Amazon affiliate links. If you click through and make a purchase, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you, as required by the Amazon Associates Program. This is how we keep the site running. Commission never decides what makes a list: a product earns its spot on merit, or it doesn't go up at all.
