Every product on Kitchen Finds goes through the same basic screen before it earns a spot on a list. Here's exactly what that looks like.

## Price verification

Every pick has to actually be available at or near the price band a post promises (under $50, under $20, etc.) at the time we write it. Amazon prices move, so if a product later drifts above the stated range we update or pull the post rather than leave a stale recommendation up. We don't chase temporary lightning deals, since a price only a handful of shoppers will ever see isn't a fair basis for a recommendation.

## What we look at

- **Verified buyer reviews.** We read the reviews on the actual retailer listing, not just the star average. A 4.5-star product with dozens of one-star reviews about a single failure mode (a lid that cracks, a motor that burns out at month three) gets flagged or dropped.
- **Real use case fit.** A product has to solve the actual problem the post is about. We don't pad roundups with filler picks just to hit a round number like "10 best."
- **Build quality signals.** Materials, warranty length, and manufacturer reputation all factor in, especially at the low end of a price range where corners get cut first.
- **Value relative to the category.** "Best under $50" means best for the money, not just cheapest. A $45 pick that lasts years beats a $15 pick you'll replace three times.

## What disqualifies a product

- No longer available, or reliably priced outside the post's stated range
- A pattern of safety complaints in verified reviews (not just isolated one-offs)
- Listings that look manipulated (review-gated, incentivized reviews, or a suspicious flood of five-star ratings in a short window)
- Counterfeit or unauthorized-reseller listings instead of the actual brand or an authorized seller

## Keeping posts current

Older posts get revisited when a product is discontinued, repriced outside its stated range, or replaced by something that's clearly a better pick at the same price. Learn more about who's behind these picks on our [About](/about/) page.
